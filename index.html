Welcome to Sesama Social Space

Sesama Social Space is more than just a café — it's a cozy, creative space where community, coffee, and conversations come together. Located in Sengon, Kabupaten Jombang, Jawa Timur, we offer specialty coffee, homemade food, and a warm ambiance perfect for working, relaxing, or meeting friends. Follow our journey, daily menu updates, and community events

Why Us

Why Choose Sesama Social Space

01

Sesama Social Space Quality

Community & Comfort At Sesama Social Space, we believe in bringing people together. Our cozy and welcoming café is designed for connection — whether you're here to work, relax, or catch up with friends, we offer a comfortable space where everyone feels at home.

02

Sesama Social Space Comfort

We take pride in serving quality coffee, thoughtfully brewed by our baristas using carefully selected beans. Whether you prefer espresso, manual brew, or blended drinks, each cup reflects our passion for great taste and consistency.

03

Sesama Social Space Variety

Beyond coffee, enjoy a wide variety of snacks, main courses, and refreshing drinks. From local comfort foods to creative beverages, our menu is designed to please every palate — all within a stylish, relaxing environment in the heart of Jombang.
